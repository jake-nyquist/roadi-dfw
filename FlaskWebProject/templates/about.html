About roadi

roadi is an app that shows any user the cheapest way to experience the events that they love in person. It uses data from StubHub and Sabre to deliver the absolute lowest prices on event admission and air travel.

roadi was created by Jake Nyquist, Brett Gutstein, and Philip Taffet at HackDFW 2015 using Bootstrap, jQuery, and Flask.
